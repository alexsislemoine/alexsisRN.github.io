Welcome to the Farm

      

        Welcome to the Farm of Feelings. This is a lively farm where children's         emotions can visit safely in gentle, caring spaces.       

      

        Here, feelings come and go. Some arrive quietly, some stomp in, and some stay         longer than we expect. On this farm, every feeling is welcomed, understood,         and allowed to leave — just as we are free to leave a visit when it is time.       

      

        No feeling is too big, too messy, or too much, because feelings are visitors,         not who we are.
