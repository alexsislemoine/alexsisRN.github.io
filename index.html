Welcome to the Farm

    

Welcome to the Farm of Feelings. This is a lively farm where childrens emotions can visit safely in safe places. Here, feelings come and go. Some arrive quietly, some stomp in, and some stay longer than we expect. On this farm, every feeling is welcomed, understood, and allowed to leave just as we are allowed to leave the visit. No feeling is too big, too messy, or too much, because feelings are visitors, not who we are.
