Farm of Feelings

Helping children understand that feelings are visitors, not identities.

Welcome to the Farm of Feelings — a gentle place where emotions can visit safely. Here, children learn that feelings come and go, and that no feeling defines who they are.

About

Alexis is a registered nurse, trauma-informed certified through Indiana University, and the creator of Farm of Feelings. Her work helps children explore emotions in safe, supportive ways while giving parents and teachers practical tools for emotional guidance.
