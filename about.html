About Farm of Feelings

Welcome to Farm of Feelings. This is a lively farm where children's emotions can visit safely in gentle, caring places on the farm.

Here feelings come and go; some arrive quietly, some stomp in, and some stay longer than we expect.

On this farm, every feeling is welcomed, understood, and allowed to leave, just as we are free to leave a visit when it is time. No feeling is too big, too messy, or too much, because feelings are visitor, not who we are.
